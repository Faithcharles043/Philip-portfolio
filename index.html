About Me.

Experienced Entrepreneur and Senior Product Manager with over 6 years of hands-on experience defining innovative business strategies and delivering high-value digital products. From founding and launching a tech startup gaining valuable lessons through its eventual failure—to building and managing fintech payment products that now process over $60 million in monthly transaction volume, I’ve led initiatives across multiple product domains in the tech ecosystem. My strengths lie in product strategy, cross-functional team leadership, and driving execution from concept to launch. I’m passionate about building user-centered, scalable solutions that solve real problems and deliver measurable impact.

I believe great products are built at the intersection of empathy, data, and execution. Whether working with startups or scaling established platforms, I focus on creating alignment between user needs, technical possibilities, and business outcomes. I enjoy mentoring teams, fostering a culture of ownership, and turning bold ideas into real-world solutions that deliver value at scale.
